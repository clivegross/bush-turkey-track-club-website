Sandgate Parkrun Speed Week 2022

SATURDAY 18 JUNE 2022 7:00 AM

Is this the fastest Parkrun event in Australia in 2022?

Sure to be one of the quickest 5km races in Queensland this year and featuring some of the fastest club runners in Brisbane. Last year saw 153 runners under 20 minutes and 28 under 16 minutes. The Bush Turkey Track Club is excited to be sharing a start line with many great local clubs and running groups, including:

  • Moreton Bay Road Runners
  • BERT Squad
  • South Pine Striders
  • Intraining
  • Start Running Stay Running
  • RunCo

A big shout out to the wonderful volunteers of Sandgate Parkrun for making this event possible.

See you on the start line. All welcome. Gobble gobble!
