Bush Turkey Relay 2024

Friday 6 December 2024 5:30 PM

  • DEAGON SPEEDWAY
  • RESULTS

The Bush Turkey Relay returns for its third year as the most anticipated event of summer 2024.

Teams of athletes each take on the 1km out and back course on the iconic Deagon Speedway, followed by a family friendly BBQ/dinner and drinks FOR MEMBERS, FAMILY and FRIENDS ONLY. $15 per athlete, $10 per spectator, kids eat free.

The relay race is open to Bush Turkey Track Club members only, but everyone is welcome to spectate. You must register to race. Spectators please also register all adults and kids to help us plan adequate catering. All welcome.
