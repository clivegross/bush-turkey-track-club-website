Bush Turkey Classic Series 2023

December 2023 to January 2024

The Bush Turkey Classic Series, a three round, balls-to-the-wall, middle distance running race series returning for its third year. Each round features a different course within the 4017 postcode of Brisbane Queensland.

Points are awarded for each of the three rounds in cross country format, lowest score wins. This year, scoring is ranked across both age category and gender category. Athletes who DNS/DNF a round are awarded the highest points for the round, plus one. Points are accumulated across the three rounds and final results for the series will be announced at the conclusion of Round Three.

WARNING: Before you register, be aware that this is an unofficial, uninsured series of running events and that there are risks associated with competitive running on public roads, pathways and bushland. You have an opportunity to walk or jog each course beforehand to identify and assess any risks. In order to participate, you must voluntarily accept that you participate at your own risk and that it is your responsibility to ensure you are fit and able to take part in each event. Be considerate to nearby members of the public and other participants at all times.
