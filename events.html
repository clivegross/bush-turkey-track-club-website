Bush Turkey Relay 2025

Friday 21 November 2025 5:00 PM

  • DEAGON SPEEDWAY
  • RESULTS

The Bush Turkey Relay returns for its fourth year as the most anticipated team event of summer 2025.

Teams of athletes each take on the 1km out and back course on the iconic Deagon Speedway, followed by a family friendly BBQ/dinner and drinks FOR MEMBERS, FAMILY and FRIENDS ONLY. $15 per athlete, $10 per spectator, kids eat free.

The relay race is open to Bush Turkey Track Club members only, but family and friends are all welcome to spectate. You must register to race. Spectators please also register all adults and kids to help us plan adequate catering. All welcome.
