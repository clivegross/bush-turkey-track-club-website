About Bush Turkey Track Club

Last updated 3 March 2024

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to empower our members to achieve their distance running performance goals, while fostering mateship and community.

Our Values

  • Performance - we fearlessly challenge our limits, we train with purpose.
  • Mateship - we are stronger together, we value our teammates, families and community.
  • Celebration - we have fun, our journey is joyful, we celebrate our teammates accomplishments, we work hard and play hard.
  • Integrity - we strive to the highest standards in our behaviour. We hold ourselves accountable and adhere to the values of our community.

Our Vision

To be the premier performance amateur distance running club in South East Queensland.
