Bush Turkey Relay 2023

Friday 10 November 2023

  • DEAGON SPEEDWAY

The Bush Turkey Relay returns for its second year as the most anticipated event of summer 2023.

Teams of athletes each take on the 1km out and back course on the iconic Deagon Speedway, followed by a family friendly BBQ/dinner and drinks. $10 per adult, kids eat free.

The relay race is open to Bush Turkey Track Club members only, but everyone is welcome to spectate and celebrate after with a family friendly BBQ/dinner and drinks. You must register to race. Spectators please also register all adults and kids to help us plan adequate catering. All welcome.
