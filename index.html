Bush Turkey Classic 2022 Series

A three round, balls-to-the-wall, middle distance running race series.

Welcome to the second year of the Bush Turkey Classic. A three round, middle distance running race series on some iconic tracks in the Bramble Bay region. This is an unofficial, uninsured and probably unacceptable race series, participate at your own risk!

Registration

You must register in the Merch section below to participate. Registration is $5 for one round or $10 for the entire series. Entry fees cover the cost of prizes and refreshments, with any remainder going toward the club AGM.

  • REGISTER

Scoring

Points are awarded per round in cross country format, corresponding to each athletes finishing position in their category, male or female. First place gets one point, second place gets two points and so on up to a maximum of five points. Athletes who DNS/DNF are awarded six points. Each race is mixed gender with a seperate female and male competition. Points are accumulated across the three rounds, lowest score wins. Final results for the series will be announced at the conclusion of round three.
