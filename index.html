The Bush Turkey Relays

FRIDAY 4 NOVEMBER 2022 6:00 PM

Bush Turkey Track Club brings you the most anticipated event of 2022.

Club members from 3 teams each run 1 length of the Deagon Speedway (one way) in an all out sprint where there can only be one winning team. Being cheered on by family and friends and followed by a bbq and beers to wash away the lactic acid and tears.

Team Captains:

  1. Leif Mawson
  2. Scott Perry
  3. James Laird

Draft night will be held prior to the event where the teams will be chosen. Standby for further details.
