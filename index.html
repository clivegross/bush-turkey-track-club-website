Bush Turkey Classic

A three round, balls-to-the-wall, short course running race series.

Welcome to the inaugural Bush Turkey Classic. A three round, short course running race series on some iconic tracks in the Bramble Bay region. As permission from local council or any other organisation will be not be sought, numbers are limited to those willing to have a red hot crack!

Scoring

Points are awarded for the three rounds in the cross country format, lowest score wins. The number of points awarded per round correspond to finishing position in each athletes gender category, up to a maximum of five points. Athletes who DNS/DNF are awarded six points for the round. Points are accumulated across the three rounds and final results for the series will be announced at the conclusion of round three.
